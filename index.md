*A young American travels to Cuba's Isla de la Juventud to find his 
grandfather's grave — and discovers a family history rooted in an 
era he never knew existed.*

---

*El Pinero* is a narrative short film written by Robert Potter and 
directed by Meilín Quilez Durañona. Shot on location on Cuba's 
Isla de la Juventud. Currently in pre-production.

My name is Robert Potter. I'm an American writer, producer, and filmmaker 
based in the United States. My work is driven by a fascination with 
history, place, and the stories that get left out of the official record.

Over the past year I've made multiple trips to Cuba's Isla de la Juventud, 
developing a deep connection to the island and its largely forgotten history. 
*El Pinero* is my second film made there, produced in collaboration with 
Cuban filmmaker Meilín Quilez Durañona, who was born and raised on the island.

I wrote the script for *El Pinero* and will be producing the film, as well 
as appearing in the lead role of Cole Smith.
