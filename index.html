--- layout: page title: El Piñero ---

A young American travels to Cuba's Isla de la Juventud to find his grandfather's grave — and discovers a family history rooted in an era he never knew existed.

El Piñero is a narrative short film written by Robert Potter and directed by Meilín Quilez Durañona. Shot on location on Cuba's Isla de la Juventud. Currently in pre-production.
